High School Final Grade Calculator

Find out what score you need on your final exam to get the grade you want in the class. Enter your current grade, how much the final is worth, and your goal.

How it works:
  • The part of your grade that is not the final is based on your current grade.
  • The final exam is weighted separately using the percentage you enter.
  • We solve for the exam score that gives you your target overall grade.
